## Thesis
Abstract wins when good apps create repeat users, streamable content, market activity, tips, trades, mints, and retention. Abracast helps identify the projects that support that flywheel and exposes the gaps that stop projects from becoming useful.

## Score Model
- User value (16%): Does it solve a real user need or create real entertainment?
- Economic activity (16%): Can it create trades, mints, tips, marketplace usage, or repeat spending?
- Replayability (13%): Will users come back after the first XP or badge moment?
- Streamer potential (13%): Can creators explain, play, review, or compete with it live?
- Onboarding quality (12%): Can a new Abstract user understand and start without heavy friction?
- Safety and trust (12%): Does it avoid unclear claims, wallet risk, broken links, and predatory mechanics?
- Update cadence (9%): Does the team ship, communicate, and improve the product?
- Abstract fit (9%): Does it use Abstract's consumer, AGW, Portal, XP, streaming, or game strengths?

## Security And Trust Gate
Projects cannot receive Support unless both ecosystem value and trust are strong. Abracast checks official source trail, wallet safety, economic clarity, audit or verification evidence, and team transparency.
- Official sources (24%): Project links should be traceable through Portal, official website, docs, GitHub, or known ecosystem directories.
- Wallet safety (24%): The app should avoid seed phrases, private keys, unclear approvals, and aggressive claim flows.
- Economic clarity (18%): Users should understand how the project makes money, spends money, or creates activity.
- Audit or verification (16%): Contracts, listings, or tools should have audits, visible source, badges, or community verification when risk is high.
- Team transparency (18%): A good project communicates updates, risks, status, seasons, downtime, and roadmap clearly.

## Source Confidence Rule
- High: official docs/site plus reliable ecosystem or third-party confirmation
- Medium: official or known ecosystem source exists, but important risk data is missing
- Medium-low / Low: directory-backed or fragmented source trail
- None: no reliable source trail or unsafe claim pattern

Support requires High source confidence, strong value, and strong trust. This prevents hype-only or under-verified apps from being promoted.
